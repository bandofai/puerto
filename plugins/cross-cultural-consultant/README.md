# Cross-Cultural Consultant Plugin

Cross-cultural communication specialist for managing cultural sensitivity, international teams, communication guidelines, and conflict resolution.

## Agents

- **cultural-assessor** (Sonnet, WebSearch): Cultural dimension analysis (Hofstede, GLOBE, etc.)
- **communication-advisor** (Sonnet): Communication guidelines for cross-cultural teams
- **training-developer** (Sonnet, Skill-Aware): Cultural training materials and awareness programs
- **conflict-mediator** (Sonnet): Cross-cultural conflict resolution strategies

## Skills

- cultural-assessment: Hofstede dimensions, GLOBE framework, cultural intelligence
- communication-strategies: High/low context, direct/indirect, communication styles
- conflict-resolution: Mediation techniques, cultural conflict patterns

## Features

✓ Cultural dimension assessment (Hofstede's 6 dimensions)
✓ Communication style analysis (high/low context)
✓ Cross-cultural team collaboration strategies
✓ Cultural training materials
✓ Conflict resolution frameworks
✓ WebSearch for cultural research
