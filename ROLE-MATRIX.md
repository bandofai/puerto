# Role Responsibility Matrix (RACI)

**Puerto Digital Agency Team - Project Lifecycle Responsibilities**

---

## RACI Legend

- **R** = Responsible (Does the work)
- **A** = Accountable (Ultimately answerable, final approver)
- **C** = Consulted (Provides input, two-way communication)
- **I** = Informed (Kept in the loop, one-way communication)

## Typical Project Team Compositions

### Small Feature (1-2 weeks)

**Core Team (3-4 people):**
- **Frontend Engineer** (R) - Implementation
- **Backend Engineer** (R) - API work
- **QA Engineer** (R) - Testing
- **Product Officer** (A) - Approval

**Consulted:**
- Design Lead, UX Writer

**Informed:**
- Engineering Lead, Marketing Director

---

### Medium Feature (1-2 months)

**Core Team (6-8 people):**
- **Chief Product Officer** (A) - Overall accountable
- **Engineering Lead** (A) - Technical accountable
- **Frontend Engineer** (R)
- **Backend Engineer** (R)
- **Data Engineer** (R)
- **QA Engineer** (R)
- **Design Lead** (R) - Design work
- **UX Researcher** (R) - User testing

**Consulted:**
- UX Writer, Accessibility Specialist, DevOps Engineer

**Informed:**
- Marketing Director, Sales Director, Chief Strategy Officer

---

### Large Initiative (3-6 months)

**Core Team (12-15 people):**
- **Chief Strategy Officer** (A) - Executive sponsor
- **Chief Product Officer** (A) - Product accountable
- **Engineering Lead** (A) - Technical accountable
- **Design Lead** (A) - Design accountable
- **Marketing Director** (A) - Go-to-market
- **Frontend Engineer** (R)
- **Backend Engineer** (R)
- **Mobile Engineer** (R)
- **Data Engineer** (R)
- **DevOps Engineer** (R)
- **QA Engineer** (R)
- **UX Researcher** (R)
- **UX Writer** (R)
- **Content Strategist** (R)
- **Data Analyst** (R)

**Consulted:**
- All other roles as needed

## Decision Rights Matrix

| Decision Type | Primary Decision Maker | Must Consult | Final Approver |
|--------------|----------------------|-------------|----------------|
| **Product Strategy** | Chief Product Officer | Chief Strategy Officer, Engineering Lead, Design Lead | Chief Strategy Officer |
| **Technical Architecture** | Engineering Lead | Chief Product Officer, DevOps Engineer | Engineering Lead |
| **Design Direction** | Design Lead | Chief Product Officer, UX Researcher | Design Lead |
| **Marketing Strategy** | Marketing Director | Chief Product Officer, Chief Strategy Officer | Chief Strategy Officer |
| **Hiring Decisions** | HR Manager | Department Lead | Operations Lead |
| **Pricing & Packaging** | Sales Director | Chief Product Officer, Marketing Director | Chief Strategy Officer |
| **Technology Choices** | Engineering Lead | DevOps Engineer, relevant engineers | Engineering Lead |
| **Brand Guidelines** | Design Lead | Marketing Director, UX Writer | Chief Strategy Officer |

### Technical Issues
```

```

### Design Issues

### Product Issues
```

```
